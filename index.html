India Signs ₹62,370 Crore Deal for 97 Tejas Fighter Jets

New Delhi: The Ministry of Defence has approved the purchase of 97 LCA Tejas Mk-1A aircraft from Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL). The move strengthens India’s air defence and gives a big boost to the “Make in India” initiative.
